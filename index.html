📌 Key Observations
  • ● ENAUTIC WaveFlyer is the only 2-seat, side-by-side electric foiler in or near production. Direct competitor to the flying couch concept. WaveDrive tech is patented.
  • ● Valo Hyperfoil targets the PWC (jet ski) replacement market — not the relaxed seating experience. Single rider, performance focus.
  • ● Candela & Navier are large, premium luxury craft ($450K–$850K). Different market segment, but their foil control IP is state of the art.
  • ● The gap: No product exists combining comfortable bench/couch seating + electric foiling + 2 passengers + price under $150K. This is the flying couch opportunity.
